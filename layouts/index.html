Simple process

Three steps to golden hour

We do the solar math. You book the ticket.

01

Choose airport & date

Get terminator scores

Scoring system

What the score means

Based on solar altitude along the full flight path.

Score Label Solar altitude What you see
90–100 🔥 Terminator crossing ±1° from horizon Deep indigo to burning orange — the full spectrum
70–89 🌅 Golden hour 1° – 6° above/below Long shadows, warm orange light, pink clouds
50–69 🌤 Partial golden 6° – 18° Blue hour, diffuse warm light, colored horizon
< 50 ☀️ Daylight / night > 18° Full daylight or complete darkness
