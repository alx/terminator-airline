## How flights are scored

Each flight receives a **Terminator Score (0–100)** based on:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 🔥 90–100 | Flight crosses terminator line mid-air |
| 🌅 70–89 | Departure or arrival during golden hour |
| 🌤 50–69 | Partial golden hour window |
| ☀️ < 50 | Full daylight or full night |

## Seat side guide

Once you find your flight, we tell you **which side of the plane to sit on** to face the terminator:

> **Rule:** The terminator always faces the sun. Sit on the side where the sun is setting or rising relative to your flight heading.

- Window seat **A/B** = left (port) side
- Window seat **F/G** = right (starboard) side
