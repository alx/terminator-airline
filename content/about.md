**Terminator Airline** is a side project born from one obsession: catching the perfect light from a plane window.

Built in Koh Samui 🇹🇭, tested across South East Asia.

The terminator line moves at ~1,670 km/h along the equator. Flying into it at 900 km/h means you have a window — sometimes literally — of maybe 20 minutes of the most extraordinary light on Earth.

This tool helps you find that window, book the right flight, and sit on the right side.
